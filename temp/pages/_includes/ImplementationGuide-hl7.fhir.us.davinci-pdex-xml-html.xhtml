<p><strong>The Payer Data Exchange (PDex) Implementation Guide (IG) is provided for Payers/Health Plans to enable them to create a Member's Health History using clinical resources (based on US Core Profiles based on FHIR R4) which can be understood by providers and, if they choose to, committed to their Electronic Medical Records (EMR) System.</strong></p>
<p>The PDex work group recognizes that changes may be required to the IG once CMS has published the final Interoperability Rule.</p>
<p>This IG uses the same Member Health History &quot;payload&quot; for member-authorized exchange of information with other Health Plans and with Third-Party Applications. It describes the interaction patterns that, when followed, allow the various parties involved in managing healthcare and payer data to more easily integrate and exchange data securely and effectively.</p>
<p>This IG covers the exchange of:</p>
<ul>
<li>Claims-based information</li>
<li>Clinical Information (such as Lab Results, Allergies and Conditions)</li>
</ul>
<p>This IG covers the exchange of this information using US Core and Da Vinci Health Record Exchange (HRex) Profiles. This superset of clinical profiles forms the Health Plan Member's Health History.</p>
<p>This IG covers the exchange of a Member's Health History in the following scenarios:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provider requested Provider-Health Plan Exchange using CDS-Hooks and SMART-on-FHIR</li>
<li>Member-authorized Health Plan to Health Plan exchange</li>
<li>Member-authorized Health Plan to Third-Party Application exchange</li>
</ul>
<p>The latter two scenarios are provided to meet the requirements identified in the CMS Interoperability Notice for Proposed Rule Making issued on February 11, 2019.</p>
