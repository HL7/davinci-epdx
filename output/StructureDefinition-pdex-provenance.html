Resource Profile: PdexProvenance

Provenance is provided by the payer to identify the source of the information, whether the data came via a clinical record or a claim record and whether the data was subject to manual transcription or other interpretive transformation.

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pdex/StructureDefinition/pdex-provenance
