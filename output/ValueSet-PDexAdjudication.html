ValueSet: PDex Adjudication (Experimental)

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pdex/ValueSet/PDexAdjudication Version: 2.0.0
Draft as of 2024-01-03 Computable Name: PDexAdjudication

Copyright/Legal: This Valueset is not copyrighted.

Describes the various amount fields used when payers receive and adjudicate a claim. It includes the values defined in http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/adjudication, as well as those defined in the PDex Adjudication CodeSystem.
